A survey of the Brigham Young University on time speading of American citizen

This is a data that Brigham Young University collected from the people who live in the United States. This Survey show the result that the most of people spend their time alone or with their partners. It is surprising that the number of counts spending with their family are much less than them. Also, most people answered that they don't speand much time with their children or friend. People should more focus on spending time with their family.



People might spend their time with watching movies, playing video games, or something their hobbies.

  1. Titanic (1997)
    • Incorporating both historical and fictionalized aspects, it is based on accounts of the sinking of the RMS Titanic and stars Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet as members of different social classes who fall in love aboard the ship during its ill-fated maiden voyage.
  2. E.T the Extra-Terrestial (1982)
    • The main message from this movie is about accepting others who are different. Values in this movie that you could reinforce with your children include friendship, loyalty, courage and selflessness.
